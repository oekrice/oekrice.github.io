Talks/Posters

UKMHD 2021 Newcastle
A poster based on the (at the time unpublished) outflow field work. The progress at this point was later proven somewhat obsolete.

Departmental Seminar June 2021 - Behaviour of Flux Ropes in a 2.5D Magnetofrictional Model
A summary of my work during the first year of my PhD. At this point it was the initial results from the first 2.5D parameter study

Departmental Seminar November 2021 - Global Coronal Equilibria with Solar Wind Outflow
A talk based on the outflow field paper.

Glasgow University A & A Seminar January 2022
An invited talk, similar to the above.

Durham and Dundee Universities MHD Research Day April 2022 - Eruptivity Criteria of 2.5D Magnetic Flux Ropes in the Solar Corona
A talk summarising on our two-dimensional flux rope research, based on the paper of the same name.

UKMHD 2022 Sheffield
A similar talk to the above. This talk was awarded the prize for second-best student talk.

NAM 2022 Warwick - Poster
A poster on our flux rope research. This was awarded a UKSP poster prize.

Departmental Seminar August 2022 - Magnetofrictional Modelling on an Icosahedral Grid
A talk outlining the creation of the new icosahedral magnetofrictional code, which at that point was almost functional in Python. More than a year later and it is almost functional in Fortran. Progress.

Durham and Dundee Universities MHD Research Day May 2023
A talk summarising the results from the new MHD study, and the comparison between the two models.

UKMHD 2023 Leeds- Eruptivity Criteria of Flux Ropes in MF and MHD Models
A similar talk to the above.

Sheffield University Plasma Dynamics Group Seminar June 2023
An invited talk, expanding on the one given at UKMHD.

NAM 2023 Cardiff- Poster
A poster on our flux rope research, inlcuding the new MHD simulations. This was awarded the UKSP special prize.

SHINE Workshop in Stowe, Vermont(!), August 2023 - Poster presentation: Eruptivity Criteria of Magnetic Flux Ropes in the Solar Corona
A new poster summarising all of the 2.5D work so far, with similar conclusions to the 2023 paper.

Departmental Seminar October 2023 - Modelling the Magnetic Field of the Solar Corona
An attempt to summarise all of my research to date as the PhD funding draws to a close, including work from all of the papers to date.

St Andrews University Solar and Magnetospheric Theory Group Seminar October 2023
An invited talk on my PhD research as a whole,similar to the Durham seminar.

NAM 2024 Hull - Invited Talk
